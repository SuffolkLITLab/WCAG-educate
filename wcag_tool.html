Contrast (Minimum) (Level AA)

Hue and saturation have little to no impact on readability, as measured by reading performance, in those without color impairments (Knoblauch et al., 1991). Luminance contrast can be slightly impacted by color deficits. So that those with color vision impairments will also have sufficient contrast between the text and the backdrop, the recommendation's contrast calculation does not take color into account.
